Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our luxury fragrances

What is the return policy for perfumes?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened perfumes in their original packaging. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund. For opened bottles, we provide a 7-day satisfaction guarantee where you can exchange for a different fragrance of equal value.

Are your perfumes 100% authentic?

All our fragrances are 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized distributors and the original manufacturers. Each bottle comes with a certificate of authenticity and is guaranteed to be genuine. We never sell counterfeit or diluted products.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $75. For orders under $75, shipping costs $9.99. We also provide express shipping options for $19.99 (1-2 business days) and overnight shipping for $29.99. All orders are carefully packaged to ensure your fragrance arrives in perfect condition.

Can I include a gift message?

Of course! We offer complimentary gift wrapping and personalized gift messages for all orders. During checkout, simply select the gift option and enter your custom message. We'll include it on elegant stationery with your beautifully wrapped package. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or any special occasion.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping delivers in 1-2 business days, and overnight shipping arrives the next business day. International shipping is available and takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination. All orders are processed within 24 hours and come with tracking information.

A Commitment To Purity

We believe in sustainable luxury. Every bottle of Odore Perla is crafted with responsibly sourced ingredients, ensuring that our passion for fragrance doesn't compromise our commitment to the environment.
